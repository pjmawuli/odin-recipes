Grilled Cheese Sandwich

Description

A grilled cheese sandwich is a simple, classic lunch or dinner that is sure to please even the pickiest eater. Learn how to make a grilled cheese sandwich in a nonstick pan with buttered bread and American Cheddar for a classic hot sandwich. This recipe is for a basic grilled cheese sandwich, but feel free to add your favorite ingredients to make it your own.

Ingredients

  • 4 slices white breadd
  • 3 tablespoons butter, divided
  • 2 slices Cheddar cheese

Steps

  1. Butter the bread on one side and place the bread butter-side down on a hot skillet.
  2. Top with cheese, then place another slice of bread on top (butter-side up).
  3. Cook until the bottom slice is lightly browned, then flip.
  4. Continue cooking until the cheese is melted and the bread is browned.
