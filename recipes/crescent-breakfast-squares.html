Crescent Breakfast Squares

Description

We love a savory breakfast, and these delicious breakfast squares fill the bill. Use ham and Swiss, or another deli meat and cheese combo, for a weekend breakfast or brunch. Prefer other veggies in your omelet? Substitute your faves!

These crescent breakfast squares are a great way to start the day. They're easy to make and are a great way to use up leftover ham. Serve with a side of fresh fruit for a complete breakfast.

Ingredients

  • 1 (8 ounce) package refrigerated crescent rolls
  • 1/2 cup shredded Swiss cheese
  • 1/2 cup chopped cooked ham
  • 1/2 cup chopped green bell pepper
  • 1/2 cup chopped onion
  • 1/2 cup shredded Cheddar cheese
  • 4 eggs
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C). Grease a 9x13 inch baking dish.
  2. Unroll crescent rolls and separate into 8 triangles. Arrange in the bottom of the prepared baking dish.
  3. Top with Swiss cheese, ham, green bell pepper, and onion. Sprinkle with Cheddar cheese.
  4. In a medium bowl, whisk together eggs, milk, salt, and pepper. Pour over the top of the crescent rolls.
  5. Bake in the preheated oven until the eggs are set, about 25 minutes.
